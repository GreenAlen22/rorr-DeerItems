# Changelog

All notable DeerItems changes.

## [3.0.14]

### Added

- **66 available items:** 24 white, 21 green, 16 red, and 5 equipment items.
- 9 new white items: Bloody Dividends, Bounty Tag, Cheat Death, Concentration, Elephant Gun, Faceted Jade, Heavy Lungs, and Tankbuster.
- 11 new green items: Assembly Blueprint, Bounty Contract, Cashback Chip, Charlatan's Die, Contagion Barb, Golden Sneakers, Laughing Fungus, Loot Fever, Magic Missile, Reserve Capacitor, and Standing Requisition.
- 12 new red items: Army Surplus, Cascade Failure, Dombra, Ingrown Idol, Leacked Capacitor, Mosquito Net, Orbital Lens, Ravenous Vine, Rift Beacon, Rust Mite, Twisted Opinion, and Violet Frenzy.
- 5 equipment items: Annihilator, Hunger, One Army Man, Soul Jar, and Tactical Visor.
- The Standing Requisition Chest interactable.
- The Cernunnos actor.
- Two artifacts: Artifact of Droneman and Artifact of Technician.
- Mod configuration: toggle each item's loot availability individually or manage all items at once.

### Changed

- Moved "Green Tea" from the white to the green tier. And "Unfocus Prisma" from the green to the white tier
- Reworked and rebalanced existing white, green, and red items, including their effects, descriptions, visual assets, and sounds.
- Expanded Russian and English localization.
- Improved content loading and added ReturnsAPI support for multiplayer.
